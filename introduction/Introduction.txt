Key Definitions
1- Population - Collection of all items of interest to our study and usually denoted with uppercase N
   - Parameters are the numbers we obtained using population
2- Sample - is the subset of population it is denoted with lowercase n 
3- Statistics are the numbers which we obtained using samples 
and this has two defining characteristics 
a. Randomness - A random sample is collected when each member of the sample is chosen from the population strictly by chance.
b. Representation - Representative sample is a subset of the population that accurately reflects the members of the entire population.
